How to Use the Klaviyo Flow Builder

This tool instantly deploys pre-built email automation flow templates directly into your Klaviyo account. Skip the hours of manual flow setup and launch proven automation in seconds:

  1. Enter Your API Key - Create a Klaviyo API key with flows:write permission
  2. Select Your Flows - Choose from proven flow templates (abandoned cart, welcome series, winback, etc.)

Why Automated Flows Are Critical

Email automation flows are the backbone of profitable email marketing programs. Here's why they matter:

  • Higher Revenue Per Email - Flows generate 3-5x more revenue per email than campaigns
  • Always-On Marketing - Flows work 24/7 without manual sending
  • Perfect Timing - Triggered emails reach customers exactly when they're most engaged
  • Better Personalization - Dynamic content based on customer behavior and data
  • Scalable Growth - Set up once, benefit forever as your list grows

Flow Setup Best Practices

  • Start with abandoned cart - Typically the highest-ROI flow for ecommerce brands
  • Customize before launching - Update copy, images, and offers to match your brand voice
  • Set appropriate delays - Don't email too soon or wait too long (abandoned cart: 1hr, 24hr, 72hr)
  • Add discount codes strategically - Use in 2nd or 3rd email, not the first
  • Test and optimize - Monitor performance and A/B test subject lines, offers, timing
  • Monitor flow metrics - Track conversion rates, revenue per recipient, and click rates monthly

Expected Performance Benchmarks

Well-optimized email flows typically achieve these benchmarks (results vary by industry):

  • Abandoned Cart: 10-15% recovery rate, 15-30% open rate
  • Welcome Series: 50-70% open rate, 5-10% conversion to first purchase
  • Post-Purchase: 40-60% open rate, 15-25% review/referral completion
  • Winback: 20-30% open rate, 3-7% reactivation rate
